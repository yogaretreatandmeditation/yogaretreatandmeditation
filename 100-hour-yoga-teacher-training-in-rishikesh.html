100 hour yoga teacher training in rishikesh, India

Embark on a Transformative 100 Hour Yoga Teacher Training at Yoga Retreat and Meditation in Rishikesh, India! This 11-day and 10-night program offers a structured and comprehensive approach, catering to both beginners and yoga enthusiasts. Our school is dedicated to providing a profound understanding of yoga, integrating body, mind, and breath practices for personal and spiritual growth.

The curriculum encompasses essential subjects such as pranayama, shat kriya, asana practices (with a focus on Hatha Yoga and Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga styles), yoga philosophy, yoga anatomy, relaxation (Yoga Nidra), and meditation. Accommodation, meals and an excursion during the retreat are included in the course fee as detailed below.

What are the Benefits of 100 Hour Yoga Teacher Training ?

The benefits of 100 Hour Yoga Teacher Training can be numerous and impactful for individuals looking to deepen their yoga practice or pursue a career in teaching yoga.A 100 Hours Yoga Teacher Training offers numerous benefits, ranging from personal growth and self-discovery to acquiring teaching skills and professional development. It empowers individuals to deepen their yoga practice, share their knowledge with others, and embark on a fulfilling journey in the world of yoga.

Here are some key advantages :

Strong Foundation

A 100 Hours Yoga Teacher Training program serves as an excellent foundation for aspiring yoga teachers. It provides a comprehensive understanding of the fundamental principles, philosophy, and techniques of yoga, setting a solid groundwork for further growth and learning.

Deepened Personal Practice

Through immersive training, participants have the opportunity to delve deeper into their own yoga practice. They can refine their alignment, explore advanced asanas (poses), and deepen their understanding of pranayama (breathing techniques) and meditation. This intensive practice can lead to increased strength, flexibility, and overall well-being.

Enhanced Knowledge

The training covers various aspects of yoga, including anatomy, physiology, yogic philosophy, and teaching methodology. Participants gain a deeper understanding of the human body and its connection to yoga poses, enabling them to practice and teach yoga in a safe and effective manner. They also learn about the ancient wisdom and principles of yoga philosophy, which can be applied both on and off the mat.

Teaching Skills

A 100 Hours Yoga Teacher Training equips participants with the essential teaching skills needed to guide others in their yoga practice. Trainees learn how to create lesson plans, sequence classes, provide modifications and adjustments, and cultivate effective communication with students. They gain confidence in their ability to lead yoga classes and inspire others on their yogic journey.

Self-Discovery and Personal Growth

Yoga teacher training is not just about becoming a teacher; it is also a transformative experience on a personal level. Participants often undergo a journey of self-discovery, self-reflection, and self-improvement. They learn to connect with their authentic selves, embrace mindfulness, and develop a deeper sense of self-awareness and compassion.

Community and Networking

Engaging in a 100 Hours Yoga Teacher Training allows participants to connect with like-minded individuals who share a passion for yoga. The training provides a supportive community where trainees can exchange knowledge, share experiences, and build lifelong friendships. These connections can extend beyond the training and offer a supportive network in the future.

Professional Development

For those interested in pursuing a career in yoga teaching, a 100 Hours Yoga Teacher Training is an essential step. It provides the necessary certification to teach yoga professionally and opens up opportunities to work at yoga studios, wellness centers, gyms, or even establish their own yoga business. The training can also serve as a stepping stone for further advanced training and specialization.

Course Certification

Upon successful course completion, you will be awarded a certificate of completion for the 100 hours Yoga TTC. It's essential to note that this 100-hour training does not count towards the required yoga teacher training hours, and it does not certify you as a yoga teacher by itself. If your goal is to become a registered yoga teacher with Yoga Alliance, you will need to complete a minimum of 200 hours of training. For those seeking Yoga Alliance registration as a yoga teacher, we highly recommend exploring our Complete TTC programs, which fulfill the 200-hour training requirement. All our comprehensive courses, including the Complete TTC programs, are recognized as Continuing Education (CE) training hours for individuals already registered as RYT (Registered Yoga Teacher) under Yoga Alliance (USA).
