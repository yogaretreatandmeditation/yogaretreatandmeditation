100 hours Aerial yoga teacher training in rishikesh

A 100-hour aerial yoga teacher training (YTT) is a more comprehensive course than a 50-hour YTT. It will give you a deeper understanding of aerial yoga and its many benefits. You will learn how to teach aerial yoga in a safe and effective way, and you will develop your own aerial yoga practice. A 100-hour aerial yoga YTT will typically cover the following topics: The basics of aerial yoga How to use the hammock or sling safely and effectively A variety of aerial yoga poses How to create and sequence aerial yoga classes Anatomy and physiology Yoga philosophy Meditation Pranayama Teaching methodology In addition to the classroom instruction, 100-hour YTTs also typically include a significant amount of hands-on practice. Students will have the opportunity to practice the asanas, pranayama, and meditation techniques they are learning, and they will also receive feedback from their teachers.

Accommodation, meals and an excursion during the retreat are included in the course fee as detailed below.

What are the Benefits of 100 Hours Aerial Yoga Teacher Training ?

Completing a 100 Hours Aerial Yoga Teacher Training program offers numerous benefits for individuals interested in deepening their knowledge and practice of aerial yoga.
A 100 Hours Aerial Yoga Teacher Training offers an in-depth understanding of aerial yoga, advanced skills, teaching techniques, safety knowledge, and artistic expression. It provides a platform for personal growth, increased physical capabilities, and career advancement in the field of aerial yoga.

Here are some key advantages :

Comprehensive Understanding

A 100 Hours Aerial Yoga Teacher Training provides a comprehensive understanding of aerial yoga, covering various aspects such as techniques, safety guidelines, teaching methodologies, and sequencing. Trainees gain in-depth knowledge of how to effectively and safely incorporate the aerial hammock into their practice and teaching.

Advanced Aerial Yoga Skills

The training allows participants to develop advanced skills in aerial yoga. Trainees learn more complex poses, transitions, and sequences that challenge strength, flexibility, and coordination. They explore a wider range of aerial techniques and maneuvers, expanding their capabilities and deepening their personal practice.

Teaching Techniques and Methodology

A 100 Hours Aerial Yoga Teacher Training focuses on developing teaching techniques specific to aerial yoga. Trainees learn how to structure and design aerial yoga classes, create engaging sequences, and adapt poses for different levels and abilities. They also gain insights into effective cueing, alignment, and modifications for aerial yoga, enhancing their ability to guide students with clarity and confidence.

Safety and Spotting

Aerial yoga involves working with suspended equipment, and safety is of utmost importance. A 100 Hours Aerial Yoga Teacher Training emphasizes safety protocols, rigging techniques, and spotting methods to ensure a secure and supportive practice environment. Trainees learn how to prioritize student safety, prevent injuries, and provide proper spotting during challenging poses or transitions.

Anatomy and Physiology

Understanding the human body in the context of aerial yoga is crucial for both personal practice and teaching. Trainees gain knowledge of the anatomical considerations specific to aerial yoga, such as the impact of gravity and suspension on the body. This understanding helps them guide students to practice with alignment, stability, and optimal body mechanics.

Artistic Expression and Creativity

Aerial yoga allows for artistic expression and creativity. Trainees explore various movement flows, artistic transitions, and performance-inspired elements. They have the opportunity to develop their own unique style and incorporate creative elements into their aerial yoga practice and teaching, fostering personal growth and self-expression.

Increased Strength and Flexibility

Aerial yoga is a physically demanding practice that builds strength, flexibility, and body awareness. Through consistent practice during the training, trainees experience improved muscular strength, enhanced flexibility, and increased kinesthetic intelligence. These physical benefits extend beyond the aerial practice and can positively impact other areas of their lives.

Community and Networking

Engaging in a 100 Hours Aerial Yoga Teacher Training allows trainees to connect with a supportive community of fellow aerial yoga enthusiasts. This community offers a valuable network for ongoing support, inspiration, and collaboration. Trainees can exchange knowledge, share experiences, and build lasting relationships with like-minded individuals.

Career Advancement

A 100 Hours Aerial Yoga Teacher Training provides trainees with an additional certification in aerial yoga, expanding their teaching repertoire and career opportunities. With this specialized training, they can offer aerial yoga classes, workshops, and private sessions, catering to a growing demand for aerial yoga instruction in various settings such as yoga studios, fitness centers, and wellness retreats.

Course Certification
