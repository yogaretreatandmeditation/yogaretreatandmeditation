200 hours yoga with 50 hours aerial
yoga teacher training in rishikesh

A 200-hour yoga teacher training (YTT) with 50 hours of aerial yoga is a great way to become certified to teach both traditional yoga and aerial yoga. Aerial yoga is a relatively new form of yoga that combines traditional yoga poses with the use of a hammock or sling suspended from the ceiling. It is a great way to improve flexibility, strength, and balance, and it can be a lot of fun. The 200-hour YTT will teach you the fundamentals of yoga, including asanas (postures), pranayama (breathing exercises), and meditation. The 50-hour aerial yoga training will teach you the basics of aerial yoga, including how to use the hammock or sling safely and effectively. You will also learn a variety of aerial yoga poses. Completing a 200-hour YTT with 50 hours of aerial yoga will give you the skills and knowledge you need to teach both traditional yoga and aerial yoga. You will be able to offer a variety of classes to your students, and you will be able to meet the needs of a diverse range of students.
